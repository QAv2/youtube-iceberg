But if you try to observe which slit it goes through, if you demand a set answer, the interference vanishes, the pattern collapses into a pair of clumps as if this particle was forced to choose. Your act of asking the question changes the answer. More than that, it creates the answer. And this is the philosophical heart of the quantum world.
